What is Buckshot Roulette?

Buckshot Roulette is a high-stakes online game inspired by the classic game of Russian Roulette. Instead of a revolver, you'll be facing a pump-action shotgun loaded with buckshot. Each round, you'll chamber a shell, point the shotgun at your head (virtually, of course!), and pull the trigger. Will you survive the round, or will you feel the **thrill** of narrowly escaping a blast?

How to Play Buckshot Roulette

Playing Buckshot Roulette is simple, but the stakes are high (virtually, of course!). Here's how to play:

  1. Click the "Play Now" button below to start the game.
  2. You will spawn in a nightclub bathroom, which you must navigate out of to meet the dealer.
  3. From there you must make decisions about weather to fire blanks or live rounds through chance.
  4. If you survive, the game will continue. If not, you'll have to start over.
